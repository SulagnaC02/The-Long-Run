# User Journey - The Long Run

## 1. Landing Page

User opens The Long Run.

The landing page explains:

"Life isn't a sprint. It's the long run."

CTA:
Start Your Long Run

↓

## 2. Google Login

User signs in using Google.

The app requests permission to:

- Google Calendar
- Basic profile

↓

## 3. AI Onboarding

Instead of filling out a long form, the AI has a conversation.

The AI gradually learns:

- Goals
- Occupation
- Timetable
- Travel time
- Sleep schedule
- Recovery routine
- Work style
- Faculty/workplace characteristics

↓

## 4. Dashboard

The dashboard shows

- Today's Pace
- Today's Schedule
- Goal Progress
- Future Relief
- Reflection Reminder

↓

## 5. Daily Check-in

The AI asks:

How are you feeling today?

- Physical energy
- Mental energy
- Stress
- Hunger

Optional:

Anything you'd like me to know before planning today?

↓

## 6. AI Planning

Gemini generates

- Timeline
- Meals
- Breaks
- Study sessions
- Buffer time

Every recommendation explains WHY.

↓

## 7. During the Day

User marks tasks complete.

If something changes,

AI replans.

↓

## 8. Reflection

Instead of

"Did you finish?"

The AI asks

- What are you proud of today?
- What made today difficult?
- Was today's schedule realistic?
- What should I remember for tomorrow?

↓

## 9. AI Learning

The AI updates

- Behavior Profile
- Motivation
- Task estimation
- Recovery needs
- Satisfaction

↓

## 10. Weekly Review

The AI writes

A weekly reflection letter

instead of only showing statistics.

The user sees

- Wins
- Patterns
- Lessons
- Suggestions
